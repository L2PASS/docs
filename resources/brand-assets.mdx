## Typography

We use a modern, clean sans-serif typeface to ensure readability across complex financial data.

* **Headings:** [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter) / Semi-Bold
* **Body:** [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter) / Regular
* **Monospace:** [JetBrains Mono](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/JetBrains+Mono) (For contract addresses and code)
