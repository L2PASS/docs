## sUSDD Recursive Strategy

The **L2Pass sUSDD Recursive Strategy** is currently live on Ethereum Mainnet. To ensure maximum transparency, we maintain a public repository of all security assessments.

### Audit Status

| Product | Auditor | Report | Date | Status |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **sUSDD Recursive Vault** | Wake Arena | [View PDF](https://github.com/L2PASS/audit/blob/main/wake-arena-usdd-morpho-vault.pdf) | Feb 2026 | <Badge color="green" icon="circle-check">Passed</Badge> |

### Audit Scope:

The Wake Arena review covered the following critical components:

- **ERC-4626 Compatibility:** Ensuring standard vault share accounting.
- **Recursive Logic:** Verification of flashloan-based looping and debt management.
- **Morpho Blue Integration:** Security of the collateralization and borrowing functions.
- **Emergency Controls:** Validation of "Safe Exit" and deleveraging triggers.
