## 1. Peg Stability Module (PSM) Risks

The strategy relies on the Ethereum PSM to convert **USDT ↔ USDD** at a **1:1 ratio** with zero slippage.

- **Administrative Fee Activation:** The USDD administrator can activate withdrawal or conversion fees (TOUT) without a timelock.
- **Liquidity Depletion:** If USDT reserves in the PSM are exhausted, converting USDD back to USDT may become unavailable, delaying forced unwinds.

> **Mitigation Strategy:**
>
> - **Real-time Monitoring:** Continuous tracking of PSM USDT liquidity.
> - **Threshold Alerts:** Alerts trigger at **10x** and **5x** total vault collateral exposure.
> - **Automated Unwind:** The vault triggers an automatic exit if PSM liquidity falls below **2x** vault exposure.

## 2. Administrative Control & Governance Risk

The USDD protocol is currently managed by an **Externally Owned Account (EOA)** without a structural timelock.

- **Immediate Execution:** Changes to fees, yield parameters (sUSDD APY), or contract logic can be executed without prior notice.
- **Operational Risk:** The admin could pause the PSM or modify sUSDD staking terms instantly.

> **Mitigation Strategy:**
>
> - **Mempool Monitoring:** Tracking of administrative transactions to detect pending changes. (_Note: Mempool monitoring and execution does not guarantee a successful position exit before a transaction confirms._)
> - **Negative Spread Protection:** The vault executes an **automated unwind** if the strategy spread becomes negative (i.e., when USDT borrow costs exceed sUSDD staking rewards).

---

## 3. Oracle & Liquidation Risk

The vault's Loan-to-Value (LTV) monitoring depends on accurate oracle pricing. The current oracle updates once per day, sourcing prices directly from the PSM 1:1 conversion.

- **Price Source Deviation:** If the price source changes or deviates from the PSM 1:1 parity, a liquidation event could be triggered on Morpho Blue.
- **High Leverage:** The strategy operates at a **0.91 LTV**, close to the **0.915** liquidation threshold.

> **Mitigation Strategy:**
>
> - **Automated Deleverage:** Immediate de-leveraging if **LTV \> 0.912** or if oracle anomalies/staleness are detected.
> - **Real-time Monitoring:** Continuous tracking of oracle health and Morpho pool oracle update proposals.

---

## 4. Yield & Spread Compression Risk

Profitability depends on a **positive spread**: `(sUSDD Yield - Morpho Borrow Rate)`.

- **Inversion Risk:** If sUSDD APY decreases or Morpho USDT borrow rates spike, the leveraged position may become unprofitable (the "cost of carry" exceeds the yield).

> **Mitigation Strategy:**
>
> - **Spread Monitoring:** Automated tracking of the net interest margin.
> - **Keeper-Triggered Exit:** Automated unwind if the borrow rate exceeds collateral yield for a prolonged period.

---

## 5. Smart Contract & Protocol Risk

The strategy interacts with a complex stack of DeFi primitives:

- **USDD & sUSDD** Staking Contracts.
- **PSM** Logic & **Morpho Blue** Market Contracts.
- **L2Pass Vault** Adapters.

> **Mitigation Strategy:**
>
> - **Institutional Standards:** Built using the audited **ERC-4626** share vault standard.
> - **Isolated Markets:** Utilization of Morpho Blue to isolate risk from other assets.
> - **Third-Party Audit:** All L2Pass specific logic has been audited by **Wake Arena**.

---

## 6. Stablecoin Structural Risk

This strategy assumes the continued stability of the underlying assets.

- **Peg Dependency:** Assumes USDT maintains its \$1.00 peg and USDD maintains operational and functional redemption parity via the PSM.
- **Systemic Stress:** Market-wide events could trigger mass withdrawals, affecting liquidity across all integrated protocols.

---

## Summary of Monitoring Controls

L2Pass maintains the following active safety triggers:

| Monitor | Action |
| :-- | :-- |
| **PSM Liquidity** | Auto-unwind if PSM USDT \< **2x** Vault Collateral. |
| **LTV Ratio** | Auto-deleverage if LTV approaches **0.912**. |
| **Spread Health** | Auto-unwind if Borrow Rate significantly higher Collateral Yield. |
| **Admin Activity** | Real-time mempool tracking for USDD Admin EOA. |
| **Oracle Status** | Monitoring for update frequency and price staleness. |

<Note>
  **User Access Risks**

  L2Pass is fully non-custodial. Users retain full control of their assets at all times. As a result, access to funds depends entirely on the security of the user’s private keys. If a user wallet becomes inaccessible, the associated staking position cannot be migrated to a new address, and funds will not be recoverable.
</Note>
