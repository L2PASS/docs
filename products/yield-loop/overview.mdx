**The sUSDD Recursive Yield Strategy** is an institutional-grade USDT Vault on Ethereum, engineered to capture capital-efficient, leveraged exposure to sUSDD. By utilizing the Morpho Blue lending protocol and the USDD Peg Stability Module (PSM), the strategy maximizes yield while maintaining a disciplined risk profile.

## Strategy Summary

**L2Pass sUSDD Recursive Yield** is a non-custodial, institutional-grade strategy that maximizes yield by capturing the spread between **sUSDD** staking rewards and **USDT** borrowing costs. Using an automated "Engine" built on the **ERC-4626** standard, the vault executes a recursive leverage loop, converting deposited USDT into sUSDD, supplying it as collateral on **Morpho Blue**, and using flashloans to multiply the position. This process amplifies native staking returns while all harvested rewards are auto-compounded back into the collateral base to increase the value of vault shares.

To protect user principal, the strategy features robust, real-time risk mitigation including automated deleveraging triggers and peg monitoring. The system continuously tracks the **1:1 conversion efficiency** of the USDD/USDT Peg Stability Module (PSM) and the net interest spread; if borrow rates spike or liquidity drops, the vault automatically unwinds positions to maintain a safe Loan-to-Value (LTV) buffer. This modular architecture ensures the strategy remains capital-efficient and defensive during market volatility, providing a transparent, rules-based alternative to traditional stablecoin farming.

## Core Architecture Layers

The L2Pass architecture is an institutional-grade, segregated framework designed for modularity and high-fidelity risk management. It utilizes a "Hub-and-Engine" topology that strictly decouples capital accounting from execution logic.

#### **A.  Accounting Layer (Hub)**

The Hub serves as the primary liquidity gateway and the single source of truth for the fund’s net asset value.

- **ERC-4626 Standard:** A fully compliant tokenized vault interface that manages the minting and burning of vault shares. Provides real-time NAV calculation by aggregating total assets deployed within the active Strategy Engine.
- **Total Capital Efficiency:** Unlike traditional models, the Hub maintains **zero idle reserves**. 100% of deposited USDT is immediately deployed to the execution layer to maximize yield generation from the first block.

#### **B. The Execution Layer (Strategy Engine)**

A dedicated logic module that executes the fund’s yield mandates without direct access to the Hub's core accounting records.

- **Recursive Spread Execution:** Orchestrates the automated sUSDD/USDT spread strategy. It manages the conversion of USDT into sUSDD and the subsequent leverage loops on **Morpho Blue**.
- **Atomic Leverage Logic:** Utilizes flashloans to achieve target exposure in a single transaction, ensuring the vault reaches its optimal yield profile instantaneously upon capital deployment.

#### **C. The Sentinel Layer**

A dedicated monitoring suite that acts as the fund's autonomous circuit breaker.

- **Peg & Rate Monitoring:** Continuously tracks the 1:1 conversion efficiency of the USDD/USDT Peg Stability Module (PSM) and the sUSDD/USDT interest rate differential.
- **Automated Intervention:** Keeper-driven logic triggers immediate deleveraging or a full "Safe Exit" if volatility, PSM liquidity, or borrow rates breach predefined safety thresholds.

## 2. Key Properties

- **Real-time Algorithmic Surveillance:** 24/7 automated monitoring of PSM liquidity, Morpho borrow rates, the USDD oracle price feed, and administrative governance activity to ensure constant protocol health.
- **Automated Unwind Controls:** Hard-coded safeguard parameters that trigger a defensive deleveraging or a total migration back to USDT if risk parameters—such as PSM depth or LTV—are compromised.
- **Zero-Slippage Execution:** By routing all primary conversions through the PSM, the vault bypasses traditional AMM fees and slippage, ensuring 100% capital efficiency for participants.
- **Native USDT Accrual:** Yield is captured directly in the Vault Share price in native USDT. The strategy avoids the volatility of "points" or inflationary reward tokens, focusing on hard-asset growth.
- **On-Demand Liquidity:** The vault operates with no lock-ups or cooldown periods. The architecture remains fully accessible for instantaneous entry and exit, reflecting a truly liquid on-chain strategy.

## Yield & Incentives

Returns within the sUSDD Recursive Yield Strategy are generated through a multi-layered approach that prioritizes capital efficiency and protocol-native rewards.

- **Recursive Spread Capture** The primary yield engine capitalizes on the positive spread between [sUSDD staking rewards](https://app.usdd.io/eth/earn) and the [Morpho Blue USDT borrow rate](https://app.morpho.org/). This spread is amplified through an automated recursive looping mechanism, maximizing the productivity of deposited capital without manual intervention.
- **Programmatic Incentives** Performance is further enhanced by external rewards (currently distributed via [Merkl](https://merkl.xyz/)). These incentives are dynamically integrated into the strategy to either bolster the underlying sUSDD yield or subsidize the market borrow costs, providing an additional layer of optimization for vault participants.
