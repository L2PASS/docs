**The sUSDD Recursive Yield Strategy** is an institutional-grade USDT vault on Ethereum, engineered to capture capital-efficient, leveraged exposure to sUSDD. By utilizing the Morpho Blue lending protocol and the USDD Peg Stability Module (PSM), the strategy maximizes yield while maintaining a disciplined risk profile.

## Strategy Summary
Capital enters and exits the vault at a strict 1:1 ratio through the PSM, ensuring zero slippage, no entry/exit fees, and continuous liquidity. Unlike traditional yield products, there are no lock-up periods, providing users with a flexible yet powerful tool for stablecoin appreciation.

The strategy targets exposure to USDD—a transparently collateralized protocol with a multi-year track record and robust ecosystem backing. 

## Key Properties

* **Structurally Anchored Pricing:** A USDD oracle price feed tied directly to the PSM for maximum stability.
* **Real-time Monitoring:** Continuous surveillance of liquidity, borrow rates, and administrative activity.
* **Automated Unwind Controls:** Algorithmic safeguards that trigger deleveraging if risk parameters are breached.

The result is a modular **ERC-4626** vault architecture that delivers operational automation and scalable, on-chain yield for the sophisticated participant.

## Yield & Incentives

Returns within the sUSDD Recursive Yield Strategy are generated through a multi-layered approach that prioritizes capital efficiency and protocol-native rewards.

---

* **Recursive Spread Capture**
  The primary yield engine capitalizes on the positive spread between [sUSDD staking rewards](https://app.usdd.io/eth/earn) and the [Morpho Blue USDT borrow rate](https://app.morpho.org/). This spread is amplified through an automated recursive looping mechanism, maximizing the productivity of deposited capital without manual intervention.

* **Programmatic Incentives**
  Performance is further enhanced by external rewards (currently distributed via [Merkl](https://merkl.xyz/)). These incentives are dynamically integrated into the strategy to either bolster the underlying sUSDD yield or subsidize the market borrow costs, providing an additional layer of optimization for vault participants.
